The Founder

Known in the community as "Coach Tiger," the founder of White Tiger Cyber brings a rare depth of experience that spans over four decades in technology. Starting with a Commodore 64 in 1984, he's been building, breaking, and securing systems since the earliest days of personal computing.

His journey into cybersecurity began in the U.S. Navy, where he served as an Electronic Warfare Technician and Cryptologic Technician — roles that built a foundation in signals intelligence and information security that few can match. This military discipline and analytical mindset carries through everything White Tiger Cyber does.

Since then, Tiger has worked at every level of IT — from designing and building networks of all sizes, to testing the security of databases, web applications, and enterprise infrastructure. He's provided security consulting and penetration testing for companies across the country, and served as a Digital Forensics and subject matter expert for law firms and private investigators in the Northwest Florida area.

Tiger has also been actively involved in the development and maintenance of Mochimo, the world's first post-quantum secure cryptocurrency — work that reflects his commitment to staying ahead of the technology curve.
